Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new room to the system?
To add a new room, navigate to the Rooms section and click the "Add Room" button. Fill in the room details including type, price, and amenities, then click "Save". The new room will now be available for bookings.
How can I generate a monthly revenue report?
Monthly reports can be generated from the Reports section. Click on the "Generate Report" button and select the desired date range. You can export the report in PDF or Excel format for further analysis.
What should I do if a booking status is incorrect?
If a booking status needs correction, go to the Bookings section, find the booking in question, and click "Edit". Update the status to the correct value and save your changes. The system will automatically notify the guest if configured to do so.

Grand Nirwana Hotel Management System

This comprehensive hotel management system is designed to streamline all aspects of hotel operations, from reservations and room management to customer relations and financial reporting. The system provides a centralized platform to manage your hotel's daily activities efficiently.

As a mockup project developed by Muhammad Abrar Rayhan, an Information Systems student at Telkom University Jakarta, this system demonstrates the potential of modern web technologies in transforming hospitality management. The project showcases key functionalities while maintaining an intuitive user interface.

Booking Management

Easily handle reservations, check-ins, and check-outs with our intuitive interface. Manage room availability in real-time and automate confirmation emails.

Real-time Analytics

Monitor your hotel's performance with comprehensive dashboards and customizable reports. Track occupancy rates, revenue streams, and guest preferences.

Customer Relationship

Maintain detailed guest profiles, track preferences, and manage loyalty programs. Enhance guest experiences with personalized services.

This demonstration version simulates the core functionalities of a production hotel management system. In a live environment, it would integrate with payment gateways, property management systems, and other operational tools to provide a complete solution for hoteliers.

Developer Note: This project represents my ongoing journey in web development and system design. As a semester 5 Information Systems student at Telkom University Jakarta, I'm continuously expanding my skills in creating practical, user-friendly solutions for real-world business challenges.
